My Story

I believe every traveller has their own brief history why they want to travel. As for me, I travel simply because I have it in my heart. Travelling satisfies my cravings for nature that is barely around Metro Manila. It also makes me wonder a lot, for there are also some places that has a great historical background.

Mostly of my trips were just a few kilometers away from the big city, and some were just around the corner. Whenever I'm on a trip, I always make sure I have my phone with me 'cause I'm gonna take a lot of pictures of the trees, the views and features of the place.

Travelling was not just about sightseeing. For me, it is a short break and a memorable escape from all the hustles and bustles. For me, it is a way of pampering myself by satisfying my wanderlust with some friends or even alone.
